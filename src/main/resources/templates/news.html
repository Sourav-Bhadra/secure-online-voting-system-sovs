Instructions

Hello everyone! Welcome to our website, where now you can cast your vote online, and that too from your home! Explore the various features of our website, and embrace the future! Read the instructions below carefully, before proceeding. And dont forget to Register, and be a part of this campain ! Thank you!

Register

To create an account, Users need to register themselves to this website. You can head to the Register Page and register yourself today!

Login

In order to login the User needs to use provide their Adhar ID and password which they provided during registration. Upon successful login, they will be redirected to the user home page. From there they can explore other features!

Voting process

This feature is accessible from the user home page, which means our user needs to be a registered user in oder to view this feature. Users will find the Voting option in the navigation bar. By clicking that user will undergo two different Verification processes and then they can cast their vote.
  • Face Verification
    Users need to provide Camera access permission for this step. They will undergo a Verification for a few seconds. If the Verification is successful they will redirected to the next page. If the Verification fails then they will be logged out.
  • OTP Verification
    After successfully completing the Face Verification, users will receive an OTP in their registered email which they need to enter.
After successfully completing all the steps, the user can cast their vote digitally and enjoy their voting rights !
We hope you have an amazing experience. Thank you !
